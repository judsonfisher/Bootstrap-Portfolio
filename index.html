About Me

My name is Judson Fisher. I was born and raised in Buffalo, New York, and I moved to the Austin area six years ago in July of 2011. I graduated from the University of Texas in May 2016 with a degree in Corporate Communication Studies. I currently study JavaScript full stack web development and hold a strong background in e-learning and business development. Experienced in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node JS, MySQL, and Express JS. Working as an Instructional Designer, I have been responsible for creating 50+ hours of e-learning curriculum for electronic security, low voltage, fire safety, and locksmith industries.


I am currently enrolled in the UT coding bootcamp with a goal of becoming a successful full stack web developer upon completion of the program. Passionate about self-education, technology, health/nutrition, and investing. In my spare time I enjoy concerts, reading, traveling, spending time outdoors, and working out.
