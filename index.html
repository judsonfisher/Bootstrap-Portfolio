About Me

My name is Judson Fisher. I was born and raised in Buffalo, New York. I moved to Austin six years ago in July 2011. I graduated from the University of Texas in May 2016 with a degree in Corporate Communication Studies.


I work as an instructional designer and am currently enrolled in the UT coding bootcamp with hopes of becoming a successful full stack web developer on completion of the classes and activities. In my spare time I enjoy concerts, reading, traveling, spending time outdoors, and working out.
